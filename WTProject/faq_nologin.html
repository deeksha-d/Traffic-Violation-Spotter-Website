1.How do I post complaints?
Login with your credentials,Upload the photo,select category of traffic violation.

2.Can I post videos or multiple photos?
No,at present only one photo may be uploaded.

3.How can I track my complaint?
You can view the status of your complaint in the Previous Complaints section.

4.Why is my complaint rejected?
Your complaint may be rejected for multiple reasons.
Registration number of the vehicle is not clearly captured
The violation is not clearly captured
Picture is irrelevant
No images captured

5.Will I be contacted by traffic authorities?
No,you will not be contacted by any traffic authorities.

6.Will the violator come to know who posted the complaint against them?
No,your name or details will not be displayed anywhere.
